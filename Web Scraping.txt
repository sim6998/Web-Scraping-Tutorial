---Web Scraping--

two parts:

1) A Web Crawler:
	- The Crawler leads the scrapper and extracts requested data.
	- The Crawler is generally called 'Spider'.
	- It is AI Technology that browse internet to index and search the content by given links.
	- It search relevent information asked by programmer.

2) A Web Scraper:
	-It is an dedicated tool that is design to extract the data from several websites quickly and effectivly.

Steps:-

1) Find URL that you want to scrape.

2) Inspecting the page.

3) Write the code.

4) Store data in the file.(csv,xml,JSON)
